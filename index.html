About The Event

Eat Your Heart Out: Poetry Theatre Production is a creative event organized by INKSTICK in partnership with FeelNHeal (A mental health and counseling firm that creatively offers mental health support). The event is a strictly artistic event that is slated to hold on Valentine Eve in the city of Maiduguri. Eat Your Heart Out: Poetry Theatre Production (Valentine Special) aims to drives the minds of the people away from war and violence, towards love and peace. Why not talk about love instead of war? Why not talk about trust instead of violence? Why not talk about break-up instead of explosions? Why not talk about romance instead of gunshots? Why not tell stories that will give hope to our young people?
